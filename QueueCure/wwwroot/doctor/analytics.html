Statistical Methodology

1. Expected Patients Tomorrow: Calculated using a Day-of-Week (DoW) weighted model. A 70% weight is applied to the historical average for this specific day of the week, blended with a 30% weight of the overall daily patient average.
2. Expected Peak Hour: Determined by grouping all historical check-in timestamps and finding the mode of the distribution (the hour with the highest patient count).
3. Expected Waiting Time: Represents the all-time arithmetic mean of the waiting duration (registration check-in to buzzer call) across all completed sessions in this room.
